About the Doctor

Dr. Muhammad Zeeshan Ahmed Raza

MBBS, MS — Andrologist

Dr. Muhammad Zeeshan Ahmed Raza is a senior Andrologist practicing in Lahore with 19 years of clinical experience. He earned his medical degree (MBBS) and completed advanced surgical training (MS) before sub-specializing in andrology — the medical discipline focused on male reproductive and urological health.

His practice covers the full spectrum of men's health concerns, from hormonal imbalances and infertility to sensitive issues that patients often hesitate to discuss. Dr. Muhammad Zeeshan Ahmed Raza is known for taking the time to listen, explain conditions in plain language, and create treatment plans that respect each patient's circumstances.
