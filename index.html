Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Men Often Ask

Straight, confidential answers — and full details on our Health Topics page.

Is everything I discuss kept confidential?
Absolutely. Every consultation is completely private. Your information is never shared, and you can discuss sensitive matters without any judgment.
Can a varicocele affect my fertility?
Yes — varicocele is one of the most common and most correctable causes of low sperm count. Many men improve their fertility after proper diagnosis and treatment.
Is erectile dysfunction just part of getting older?
No. In most men there is a real, treatable cause such as poor blood flow, diabetes, stress, or low testosterone — and it can be an early warning sign of heart disease, so it's worth addressing.
I'm getting married soon and feel anxious — can you help?
Yes, this is one of the most common reasons younger men book a visit. A short, confidential consultation for accurate information, reassurance, and treatment of any real issue makes a big difference.
Do you offer online consultations?
Yes — secure online video consultations are available by appointment for Rs. 2,500, so you can get expert advice privately from anywhere in Pakistan.
