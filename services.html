Andrology & Men's Health

Complete Range of Services

Confidential consultation and modern treatment for the most common — and most sensitive — men's health concerns.

Erectile Dysfunction

Detailed evaluation including hormonal, vascular, and lifestyle factors. Treatment options range from oral medications to advanced therapies.

Hormonal Disorders

Testosterone deficiency (low T), andropause, thyroid-related issues, and other endocrine concerns evaluated through structured lab workups.

Male Infertility

Comprehensive fertility assessment — semen analysis, hormone testing, scrotal evaluation — plus tailored treatment for couples planning pregnancy.

Premature Ejaculation

Confidential assessment and combination treatment using behavioural techniques, topical agents, and oral medication where indicated.

Prostate Health

Screening and management of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), prostatitis, and prostate-related urinary symptoms.

Urological Concerns

Recurrent urinary infections, varicocele, hydrocele, and other urological conditions with surgical referrals when needed.
